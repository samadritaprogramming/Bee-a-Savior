Why Bees Matter

Small pollinators. Massive impact. Without bees, our ecosystems and food supply would look very different.

Why Are Bees Important?

🍎

Food Production

Bees pollinate fruits, vegetables, nuts, and countless crops that people depend on every day.

🌸

Biodiversity

Pollination helps wildflowers and native plants reproduce, supporting entire ecosystems.

🌎

Healthy Planet

Thriving bee populations contribute to stronger ecosystems and healthier environments.

Threats Facing Bees

🏗️

Habitat Loss

Urban development reduces the number of safe places for pollinators to live.

🧪

Pesticides

Some chemicals can negatively affect bee health and their ability to pollinate.

🌡️

Climate Change

Changing temperatures can disrupt flowering seasons and bee activity.
